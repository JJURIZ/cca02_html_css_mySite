Welcome to the Cat-A-Log

Cats are cool. Cats have personality. Sometimes their personalities are annoying. Sometimes they are loving. Cats are unpredictable.

I never expected to co-habitate with cats. I grew up with dogs. Yet over the last 20 years I've had the pleasure of getting close to 9 cats. To learn about my experiences with each of the 9 cats click on the links above. Each page contains images, descriptions, and my top moments with each cat.

Cats are like onions: they stink. And they have layers. Maybe cats are like ogres.
