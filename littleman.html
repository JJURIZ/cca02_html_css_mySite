Cat-A-Log Item: Little Man

Little Man was a wee guy when we got him.

It was late afternoon and we stopped by a pet store to get a collar for one of our dogs. We walked up right as they were closing and right as a pair of military men were attempting to give a two week old kitten to the employee. She refused. They asked us and of course we said yes.

His eyes weren't even open yet. That was nearly 19 years ago. As of this writing (late 2019) he's still with us.

He is unquestionably the strangest cat we've owned. He's had several behavior unsolvable behaior issues. But then he will also sweetly lie on my chest before bed.
